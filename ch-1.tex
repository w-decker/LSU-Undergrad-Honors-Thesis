A complex morlet wavelet can be derived from the following equation.

%%%% Equation %%%%
\begin{equation}
\label{gausswindow}
    w=e^{2i \pi ft}e^\frac{-t^2}{2\sigma^2}
\end{equation}

Equation \ref{gausswindow} is the product of a sine wave and gaussian window.
